Why Us ?

God cares for you, and so do we.

We at AIC Neema Wilson Airport Church stand on the basis of Biblical authority. We offer hope through the salvation of Jesus Christ. Community is very important to us, and we strive to offer a supportive environment where fellowship is reality. We are committed to serving our brothers through evangelism, sharing God's love beyond our walls...

Morning Glory

Join us for Morning Glory from 8 AM to 9 AM, where we come together to worship, pray, and close with a brief sermon.

From 10 AM to 12:30 PM, experience a service filled with God's power and anointing.
