About us

AIC-NEEMA WILSON AIRPORT CHURCH

Welcome to AIC Neema, located near Wilson Airport along Langata Road, Nairobi. We are close to the Carnivore Grounds, making it easy to find and visit. Join us for worship and fellowship!

Keep Your Faith

Live by Faith

2 Corinthians 5:7 For we live by faith, not by sight. Hebrews 11:6 And without faith it is impossible to please God, because anyone who comes to him must believe that he exists and that he rewards those who earnestly seek him.

Love one another

John 13:34-35. “A new command I give you: Love one another. As I have loved you, so you must love one another. By this everyone will know that you are my disciples, if you love one another.

Be courageous

Joshua 1:9 .Have not I commanded thee? Be strong and of a good courage; be not afraid, neither be thou dismayed: for the Lord thy God is with thee whithersoever thou goest

Seal of God

Jesus said, “Labour not for the meat which perisheth, but for that meat which endureth unto everlasting life, which the Son of man shall give unto you: for Him hath God the Father sealed.” John 6:27 Therefore, it is only by being in Christ that you can be sealed.

Our Services

Sunday Services

Morning Glory

Join us for Morning Glory from 8 AM to 9 AM, where we come together to worship, pray, and close with a brief sermon.

Main service

From 10 AM to 12:30 PM, experience a service filled with God's power and anointing.

Youth service

Happening immediately after the main service, this is a time for the youth to gather and share what is meaningful to them, ends at 1:30 PM.
