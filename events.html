Events

Our church has a number of different engaging programs that appeal to various segments of the society. We have men’s and youth retreats where the objectives are spiritual growth, nurturing fellowship, and meditation. Camps for the women provide room for glorifying God, education and self upliftment. Additionally, we have graduation ceremonies, where we honor and reward achievements as well as encourage further development in God’s ways, on top of celebrating important milestones. Every one of these activities presents an opportunity to the members to relate, develop spiritually and foster friendship.
