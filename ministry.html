Why Us

God cares for you, and so do we.

We at AIC Neema Wilson Airport Church stand on the basis of Biblical authority. We offer hope through the salvation of Jesus Christ. Community is very important to us, and we strive to offer a supportive environment where fellowship is reality. We are committed to serving our brothers through evangelism, sharing God's love beyond our walls...

Biblical Authority

Romans 13:1: “Let everyone be subject to the governing authorities, for there is no authority except that which God has established”.

Salvation through Christ

Romans 6:23 'For the wages of sin is death, but the gift of God is eternal life in Christ Jesus our Lord.

Community and Fellowship

1 John 1:7 But if we walk in the light, as he is in the light, we have fellowship with one another, and the blood of Jesus, his Son, purifies us from all sin

Mission and Evangelism
